Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to use the Lost & Found system effectively.

  1. Step 1: If you’ve found an item, go to the Report Item page.
  2. Step 2: Fill in the details — location, description, date found, and upload a clear photo.
  3. Step 3: Once submitted, the admin will verify and publish the item on the site.
  4. Step 4: If you’ve lost something, browse the Browse Items section.
  5. Step 5: Found your item? Click the “Claim” button and provide your contact info to request verification.
  6. Step 6: Wait for an admin email with next steps for item pickup.

Tip: Always check the site frequently — new items are added regularly!
