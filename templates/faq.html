Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Lost & Found @ Hightstown.

How do I report a found item?

Go to the Report Item page, fill out the form with item details, and upload a photo. Once submitted, an admin will review and approve it.

How can I find my lost item?

Visit the Browse Items page. You can search by category, description, or date found.

How does the claim process work?

When you see your lost item listed, click “Claim” and complete the claim form. Our team will contact you once your claim is verified.

Who manages this website?

This website was created and maintained by the Hightstown High School FBLA Web Development Team for the 2025–2026 competition year.
