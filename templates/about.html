What We Do

Welcome to the Lost & Found @ Hightstown website! We are a dedicated service for students of Hightstown High School to help reunite lost items with their owners. Whether you've lost something in the classroom, cafeteria, gym, or anywhere on campus, our platform makes it easy to report and find lost belongings.

Our mission is to provide a simple, efficient, and transparent way for students to reclaim their lost items. By reporting found items and checking our database, you can help reduce lost property and ensure that valuable items find their way back to you.

This service is operated by Vihaan Mody & Shourya Pendgaonkar and is free for all Hightstown High School students to use. Together, we can create a more connected and caring school community!
